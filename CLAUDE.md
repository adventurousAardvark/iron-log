## Training Constraints & Preferences

### Programming Style
- Progressive overload, compound movement focus
- Pattern: fast initial strength gains → slower incremental weight increases over time
- Use double progression (reps first, then weight)

### Exercises to AVOID
- Any movement that aggravates **distal bicep tendinopathy** — avoid heavy supination under load, heavy curls, heavy chin-ups initially
- **Barbell bench press** — right shoulder has clicking and pain
- Dumbbell press is OK

### Injuries / Rehab Targets
1. **Distal bicep tendinopathy (diagnosed)** — needs progressive rehab protocol integrated into program
2. **Right shoulder** — clicking + pain on bench press; rehab goal is return to barbell bench press

### Rehab Sources
- Use peer-reviewed journal sources from the **last 20 years** only

---

## Diet Constraints & Preferences

- **Goal:** Lower LDL and total cholesterol
- **Protocol:** Leangains (Martin Berkhan) — 16:8 intermittent fasting, higher calories/carbs on training days, lower on rest days
- **Protein:** High — target ~1g per lb bodyweight
- **Food variety:** Minimal — Pradeep prefers repeatable, simple meals
- **Foods:** To be provided by Pradeep (fridge contents / preferred foods)
- **Diet sources:** Peer-reviewed journals from the **last 15 years** only

---

## Activity Targets
- Minimum **6,000 steps/day** or equivalent cardiovascular activity
- This is IN ADDITION to gym workouts
- Recommendations should be practical and varied (walking, cycling, etc.)
